Welcome to JUNE-NZ
===================================

**JUNE-NZ** is a collobrative Artificial Intelligence (AI)-enabled Agent-Based Modeling (ABM) framework for public health modeling. The model has evolved from the world-leading **JUNE** model, 
originally developed by Durham University (UK). This collaborative framework incorporates cutting-edge AI technologies to facilitate dynamic simulations and analyses of complex systems in the context of New Zealand.

This document gives an overview of JUNE-NZ, and the steps of runing the workflow.

.. note::

   **JUNE-NZ** is an ongoing project from `Inisitute of Environmental Science and Research (ESR), New Zealand <https://www.esr.cri.nz/home/about-esr/>`_ 

The codes of **JUNE-NZ** can be accessed from `here <https://github.com/jzanetti/GradABM_ESR>`_
